The Journey of Life Cut Short


The poverty, hunger, and violence in her hometown causes Maria's family to undertake
the perilous journey to the US in order to seek better and safer living condition.

In many cases, unaccompanied children founded at the border started the
journey with their parents and were separated during the long dangerous trip.

Maria is one of nearly 130,000 unaccompanied migrant children that entered the U.S. shelter system in 2022.
This was a record.
